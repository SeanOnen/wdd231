Site Plan: Renewable Energy Systems in Agriculture

Site Name

Renewable Energy Hub

This name clearly communicates the site’s focus on renewable energy technologies used in agriculture. It is short, descriptive, and suitable for educational or informational branding.

Site Purpose

The website provides clear, accessible information about how renewable energy—solar, wind, and bioenergy—is used in modern agriculture. It offers explanations, examples, benefits, and tools to help visitors understand how clean energy improves sustainability, efficiency, and cost savings on farms.

Scenarios

  • “What renewable energy options are best for powering irrigation systems on my farm?”
  • “How does a biogas digester work, and what equipment is needed to install one?”

Color Scheme

Chosen colors reflect nature, sustainability, and clean energy.

  • Primary Green (#2b7a2b): Used for headings, accents, and key highlights.
  • Light Green-Tint (#f4f9f4): Used as the page background to maintain a clean, eco-friendly aesthetic.
  • Dark Green (#1a2e1a): Used for body text for readability and thematic consistency.

Typography

  • Montserrat: for headings (clean, strong, modern).
  • Lato: for paragraph text (simple, readable, friendly).
